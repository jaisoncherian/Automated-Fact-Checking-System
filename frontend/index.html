Output Classification

Three Verdict Types

VeriNews AI categorizes every input into one of three clear, actionable outcomes.

✅
TRUE
The claim closely matches a verified, trusted fact in our database. Confidence score is high and the source is credible.
❌
FAKE
The claim contradicts verified facts or closely matches known misinformation patterns. Treat with strong skepticism.
⚠️
SUSPICIOUS
The claim is partially similar to known facts but contains distortions, missing context, or unverified additions.
